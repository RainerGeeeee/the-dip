Model

Monthly output of the people in scope follows a dip-recovery curve (refs. 8, 1-4): unmanaged bottoms out at about 87% of a normal month around month 7 and settles at 96%; engaged bottoms out at about 96% around month 3, is back at 100% by month 8 and settles at 103%. Totals are cumulative: for each of the 36 months after move-in, (curve % − reference %) × the monthly value, added up; the unmanaged headline is measured against today's 100%, the engaged headline against the unmanaged curve, with both curves' own dips included in their sums. Output is converted one to one into the value carried (every 1% = the figure shown under the chart). Scenario depths are conservative planning assumptions consistent with refs. 1-7; the € 274,7 M basis is derived from Roche’s published Group figures (€ 66,9 bn ÷ 112.774 × 463) as documented in the calculation note above.
